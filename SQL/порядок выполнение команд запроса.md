# порядок выполнения операторов

```sql
   FROM/JOIN
       |
       v
     WHERE
       |
       v
    GROUP BY
       |
       v
    DISTINCT
       |
       v
     SELECT
       |
       v
     HAVING
       |
       v
    ORDER BY
       |
       v
   LIMIT/OFFSET
```

         v

         |
